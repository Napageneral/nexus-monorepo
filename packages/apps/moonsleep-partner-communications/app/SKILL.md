# MoonSleep Partner Desk

Use this app to inspect immutable Alibaba or Gmail communication evidence and
to project operator-reviewed supplier open loops. Keep provider-native threads
separate. Categories are facets, not lifecycle state. Model proposals do not
enter the operational queue until reviewed.

The shared Alibaba/Gmail projector and both `record.ingested` subscriptions install dormant.
Do not enable continuous work or any reply authority without a separately
reviewed activation receipt.
