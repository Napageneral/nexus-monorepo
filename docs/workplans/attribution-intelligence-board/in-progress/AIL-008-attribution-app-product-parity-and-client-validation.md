# AIL-008 Attribution App Product Parity And Client Validation

## Goal

Take the Attribution Intelligence app from substrate-complete to product-ready
by porting the most valuable MoonSleep attribution reads and validating the
result on live hosted client runtimes.

## Acceptance

1. the parity work is tracked on a dedicated attribution app board
2. the app closes the highest-value MoonSleep product gaps without regressing
   the reusable architecture
3. hosted MoonSleep proves the upgraded product surface on the currently live
   stack
4. Devenir becomes the next real client proof for the shared attribution app
