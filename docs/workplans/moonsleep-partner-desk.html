01 / Decision

Conversations are evidence. Open loops are the work.

A single supplier conversation can carry dozens of independent questions, promises, samples, costs, schedules, defects, decisions, and follow-ups. Treating the conversation as one ticket loses the actual operational state.

We preserve every provider-native thread.

Alibaba and Gmail messages remain immutable, source-bound records with exact revisions, attachments, timestamps, direction, and provenance.

We track each open loop independently.

Every loop has its own evidence, owner, lifecycle, next action, follow-up time, labels, and closure proof.

Categories such as product specification, pricing, shipment, payment, quality, or sample are useful facets. They are not the queue object and they do not decide whether work is open or closed.

The browser capture is the source boundary because it is the proven path to complete Alibaba history. Authentication is isolated from record normalization. A capture can fail or require reauthentication without corrupting the last completed snapshot or advancing Nex state.

Historical backfill and continuous capture use the same adapter contract. At-least-once overlap is expected; exact external record IDs and revision digests make replay safe.

07 / Acceptance

“Done” means production truth, not source readiness.

  • Every adapter record binds a provider message revision and sanitized source receipt.
  • Complete Surewal capture includes messages and attachment custody.
  • Second Surewal ingest creates zero records, contacts, entities, events, jobs, or projections.
  • Complete Alibaba backfill reconciles every completed native conversation and message.
  • Second full backfill creates zero durable duplicates.
  • Every in-scope source message has an explicit coverage disposition.
  • One message may update several reviewed open loops.
  • Cross-channel loops preserve independent Alibaba and Gmail threads.
  • Resolved loops retain exact closure evidence and history.
  • Model-only identity and loop proposals remain outside operational truth.
  • Production cohort counts and hashes match source receipts.
  • Restart and replay do not lose or duplicate work.
  • Authentication expiry stops capture safely without harming prior state.
  • Partner Desk cannot mutate PO, shipment, inventory, payment, or product systems.
  • Continuous capture is bounded, observable, pausable, and reversible.
  • Sending remains absent until the separate authority phase is approved.
08 / Authority

Useful immediately. Dangerous only by explicit design.

Initial production authority

Read completed capture snapshots. Ingest immutable records. Resolve reviewed partner identity. Propose and review open loops. Display source-linked operational context.

Explicitly absent

No automatic reply. No purchase-order change. No shipment or routing change. No inventory mutation. No payment or invoice action. No customer promise. No unreviewed identity merge.

The later reply phase is intentionally separate. It adds drafting before sending, explicit recipient and provider review, exact operator confirmation, rate limits, provider-scoped credentials, durable receipts, and post-send readback. It does not expand into other operational authority.
